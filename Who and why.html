WHO AND WHY

We live in societies suffocating in waste. Our task is to remind people of the richness and complexity in nature; to protect nature as far as
we can and by doing so we ensure that the future generations will never forget the scent of the flower and the utility of plants because
mother nature gave us everything we know today and we should thank it for that.

Me and my brother, this site was inspired by Gustav Metzger's fight for nature; because he wanted to let people know how complex, rich and dazzling nature was....
